Our Story

Founded in 2010, Éclat began as a passion project between Chef Kishan. What started as a small 12-table bistro has blossomed into one of the city's most celebrated dining destinations.

Our philosophy is simple: source the finest seasonal ingredients and let their natural flavors shine through thoughtful preparation. Each dish tells a story of tradition meeting innovation.

The name "Éclat" reflects our commitment to moments of brilliance - in food, wine, and service. We invite you to experience this vision in our intimate, art-filled dining room.
